Maggie

Description

Maggi Noodles is a quick and easy instant noodle dish loved by people of all ages. Made with wheat-based noodles and a signature spicy masala seasoning, it takes just a few minutes to cook. The noodles are boiled in water, mixed with the special Maggi masala, and cooked until they reach a soft yet slightly chewy texture. Simple yet delicious, Maggi can be enjoyed plain or customized with vegetables, eggs, cheese, or spices for extra flavor. Whether as a quick snack, late-night comfort food, or a lazy meal option, Maggi is a go-to favorite for many! 🍜😋

ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1 packet Maggi masala (comes with the noodles)
  • (Optional) 1 tsp butter/ghee for extra taste

Steps

  1. Boil Water – Heat 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add Noodles – Break the Maggi noodles into the pan (or keep them whole if you like long strands).
  3. Cook the Noodles – Let them soften for 1-2 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Add Masala – Sprinkle the Maggi Masala and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes until the water reduces to your desired consistency.
  5. Serve & Enjoy – Transfer to a bowl, add butter (optional), and eat hot! 😋
